Progression Phases
1–2
Form Only
Hold weight. Perfect every rep. Should feel almost too easy.
3–4
First Real Loads
+5 lbs on big lifts / +2.5 lbs dumbbells when all reps hit.
5–6
Strength Builds
+5 lbs when all sets complete with good form.
7–8
Find the Ceiling
Push to form breakdown. Record max working weights.
